V4 Technology

Desmosedici Stradale V4 Engine

The 90° V4 configuration with counter-rotating crankshaft delivers explosive power with perfect balance. Derived directly from MotoGP technology, this engine represents the pinnacle of Ducati's engineering.

  • 214 hp @ 13,000 rpm (Panigale V4)
  • 12,600 rpm redline
  • Counter-rotating crankshaft for enhanced handling
  • Desmodromic valve system for maximum performance

Electronics Package

Cornering ABS, traction control, wheelie control, launch control, engine brake control, and multiple riding modes.

Chassis Dynamics

Aluminum monocoque frame, Öhlins suspension, and Brembo Stylema brakes for ultimate control.

Aerodynamics

Winglets generating 30kg of downforce at 270 km/h to keep the front wheel planted.
